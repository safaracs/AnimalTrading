Smallest Chameleon

Brookesia micra is a species of chameleon. It is the smallest known chameleon and among the smallest reptiles.

  • Scientific Name: Brookesia micra
  • In Length: can grow up to 29 mm
  • Place of Origin: Madagascar
  • Hobby: Tree hugging

They typically reside in leaf litter during the day, and climb up into tree branches as high as 10 cm (3.9 in) at night to sleep.
